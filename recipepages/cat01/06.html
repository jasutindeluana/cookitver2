Pinakbet

Ingredients

Kosher salt

1 bitter melon (about 225g) cut into 1/4-inch-thick slices (optional)

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 small yellow onion (4 ounces), finely chopped

2 medium garlic cloves, minced

1/4 cup ginisang bagoong (see note)

2 cups homemade or instant dashi

1 small sweet potato (about 225g), cut into 1/4-inch slices

8 ounces Chinese long beans or string beans, cut into 2-inch lengths

1 medium Japanese eggplant (about 170g), cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices

8 ounces (225g) okra, caps trimmed, pods cut in half on a bias

Cooked white rice, for serving

2 plum tomatoes (about 225g), cored and chopped
